Here, you can find the most similar records from the GEO Database. To do that, you have to put in a particular record's ID, and optionally select the number of similar records to show.
For instance, in record 
https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/sites/GDSbrowser?acc=GDS5879
the input number is 
GDS5879
.
The GSE records are also supported: enter 
GSE425
for 
https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE425
.
